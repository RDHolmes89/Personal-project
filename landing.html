What's the purpose?

There is no secret that the United States and the world has needed considerable mending of it's infrustructure. Especially the often over looked need for wildlife crossings. There is no perfect time than the present to get involved and preserve life anyway possible. Recently Congress passed the bipartisan infrastructure package given to all 50 states to help implement wildlife crossings nationwide. With the allotted $350 million from the wildlife crossing pilot program included in the package, we can finally get more serious about this problem. There are more than 1000 wildlife crossings in the U.S. and I believe more are needed. The most common crossings are bridges, overpasses, tunnels, viaducts and culverts. Tag Terra is an interactive way to get involved by mapping out your own wildlife crossings!
